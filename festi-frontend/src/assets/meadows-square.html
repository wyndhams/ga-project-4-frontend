    Meadows in the Mountains Festival, 2nd – 5th June 2022

    Meadows in the Mountains Festival Lineup: To be announced!

    Meadows in the Mountains Festival is unlike any other festival in the world. Just imagine arriving to a place where the land touches the sky. To a secret paradise nestled in the clouds. A place where the summer dew glistens every colour of the rainbow against the breathtaking morning sunrise, where magic and adventure converge. A secret space full of like minds, open hearts and unforgettable memories.

    The Meadows in the Mountains Festival is one of the world’s most difficult and logistically challenging festival environments possible. The festival has evolved from a small party to an international community.

    But Meadows in the Mountains is not just a festival, it’s truly a full body experience. If you think you’re just going for some DJ sets and a boogie, you’re sorely mistaken. On the top of the mountain, open yourself up to sound healing, Bulgarian folklore classes, storytelling, open mic sessions, foraging workshops and much more.

    Come and see for yourself how it feels to party up in the mountains!

    WHEN: FROM 2nd June TO 5th June 2022

    WHERE: Polkovnik Serafimovo, Bulgaria
